Bofrot (Togbei)

A Ghanaian snack that is crispy outside and soft inside. Sweet, warm, and perfect for breakfast or a quick treat.

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes + 1 hour rising
  • Cook Time: 10–15 minutes
  • Servings: 10–12 balls
  • Difficulty: Medium

Recipe Image

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 teaspoons yeast
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup warm water or milk
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, nutmeg, and yeast.
  2. Whisk egg with warm water or milk.
  3. Combine everything into a smooth batter.
  4. Cover and let the batter rise for about 1 hour.
  5. Heat oil and fry spoonfuls of the batter until golden brown.

Tips

Fry on medium heat so the inside cooks well. If the oil is too hot, the bofrot will brown too quickly.
